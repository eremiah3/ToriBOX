HOW TORIBOX PAYS CREATORS

ToriBOX is built for one purpose: to empower African storytellers
and help them earn from their creativity.

That's why every creator on ToriBOX earns from two streams:

  1. Subscription Streaming
  2. ToriBOX Coins (Pay-As-You-View)

And we give 60% of all ToriBOX revenue back to creators.

1. Earn When People Watch Your Films

Every minute a paying subscriber watches your content earns you money.

How it works:

  • We take 60% of all subscription revenue
  • We calculate total minutes watched across the platform
  • You get paid your share based on your minutes

Example:

If your film accounts for 10% of all minutes watched, you earn 10% of the subscription creator pool.

No popularity needed. No follower count required. Every creator earns.

2. Earn When Fans Use Coins to Unlock Episodes (PAYV)

Users buy Coins to:

  • Watch individual episodes
  • Unlock premium stories

Every time someone uses Coins on your content:

  • You earn 60% of the Coins revenue
  • ToriBOX keeps 40% to keep the platform running

It's like "pay as you view" but creators earn more than traditional PPV.
